Timeline / Milestones

Milestone 1 (Weeks 1–3)
Sourcing & most viable product listing: confirm supplier, order samples, create basic Amazon listing and product photos.
Milestone 2 (Weeks 3–5)
Branding & Marketing: finalize logo/images, write listing copy, set up Seller Central and PPC structure.
Milestone 3 (Weeks 5–7)
Import & Inventory: ship first commercial order, clear customs, create SKUs, and receive inventory at fulfillment center.
Milestone 4 (Weeks 7–9)
Soft Launch & Optimize: run initial promotions, gather feedback and reviews, optimize listing and ad campaigns.
Milestone 5 (Weeks 9–12)
Full Launch & Scale: increase marketing spend, expand promotions, monitor KPIs and reorder inventory.
